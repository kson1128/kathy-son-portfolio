TAGGED:
One of the standout features of Tagged is its integration with AWS S3, a reliable and scalable cloud storage service. This allows users to easily upload and store their graffiti artwork, ensuring that their creations are safely archived and accessible anytime, anywhere. The utilization of AWS S3 enables the app to handle a large volume of graffiti uploads without compromising performance or user experience. The app also boasts an extensive gallery where users can explore and admire the graffiti art of fellow artists. By logging in, users can discover inspiration. Tagged utilizes Redux, Express, and PostgreSQL to create a seamless and efficient experience, providing users with a smooth navigation through the app's features.
Tech Stack:
React-Native | Redux | Express | PostgreSQL | AWS S3 | ViroReact | Sequelize | React-Native Router | JWT | bcrypt
